My Journey

From curious beginner to passionate developer

The Beginning

My journey into technology began with curiosity and a simple question: "How do computers really work?" What started as tinkering with basic HTML pages quickly evolved into a deep fascination with the endless possibilities of software engineering.

The moment I wrote my first Python script that automated a tedious task, I knew I had found my calling. The ability to solve real-world problems through code felt like having a superpower, and I've been hooked ever since.

Learning & Growth

My formal education in Computer Science provided a strong foundation, but the real learning happened through countless hours of building projects, debugging code, and collaborating with fellow developers. Each challenge taught me something new, whether it was a technical skill or a valuable life lesson about persistence and problem-solving.

The MLH Fellowship has been a transformative experience, exposing me to industry-standard practices, collaborative development, and the importance of building reliable, scalable systems. It's where theoretical knowledge meets real-world application.

What Drives Me

Technology has the power to create positive change in the world, and I want to be part of that impact. Whether it's building accessible web applications, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring newcomers to the field, I believe in using my skills to make a difference.

I'm particularly passionate about creating inclusive, user-friendly experiences and writing clean, maintainable code that others can build upon. The best solutions are not just functional ‚Äì they're elegant, accessible, and built with the user's needs at the center.
